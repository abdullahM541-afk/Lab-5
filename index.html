Welcome to Strike a Chord

At Strike a Chord, we believe that everyone has the potential to make music. Our professional instructors offer personalized lessons in piano, guitar, and violin for students of all ages and skill levels. Whether you’re picking up an instrument for the first time or refining your skills, we’ll help you find your rhythm.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire creativity, confidence, and a lifelong love of music in every student. We strive to make learning fun and rewarding through one-on-one instruction, group classes, and performance opportunities.

Our Lessons

  • Piano: Learn classical, jazz, or pop styles with customized lessons.
  • Guitar: Master acoustic or electric techniques from expert musicians.
  • Violin: Develop precision, tone, and performance confidence.
